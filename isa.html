Slab Design

ISA slabs are functional with a clean, tamper-evident case. The label is clear but basic.

Submission & Pricing

Standard turnaround time: 5–10 business days

Base grading price: $12/card

Transparency & Technology

ISA offers a public pop report and API access for developers.

QR code verification included. No AI or blockchain features currently used.

Resale Performance

Lower resale comps compared to PSA/SGC. More suited for personal collections than flipping.

Brand Visibility

Moderate social media presence. Limited presence at major card shows.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Fast turnaround, affordable pricing, strong pop report
  • Cons: Lower resale value, basic label design

Final Take

ISA is a solid budget-friendly grader with fast service and strong pop data. Best for collectors who want quick, affordable grading over resale performance.
