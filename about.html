About SlabMetrics

SlabMetrics was built with one goal: to provide a clear, honest look at the sports card grading landscape. As a collector and analyst, I saw a gap in the hobby—too much noise, not enough clarity. So I set out to build a platform that could evaluate grading companies based on metrics that matter.

Whether you’re grading for resale, collecting for the long haul, or just comparing slabs for fun, SlabMetrics is your go-to resource. We focus on transparency, turnaround times, resale value, appearance, and more—without the hype.

You won’t find paid placements here. We highlight everyone from PSA and SGC to newer entrants like TAG, Arena Club, and ISA. Each profile is carefully researched, community feedback is welcomed, and rankings are updated to reflect actual hobby movement.

This platform isn’t corporate. It’s a passion project rooted in collecting, coaching, sports analytics, and a desire to make card grading more accessible to everyday hobbyists.

Over time, SlabMetrics will evolve. Expect deeper tools, grading calculators, sales data comparisons, and even a slab design showcase. But through it all, the foundation remains the same: clean insights, zero bias.

Thanks for checking it out — and welcome to a smarter way to collect.
